ZendIQ provides swap routing analysis and optimisation. Not financial advice. Use at your own risk. Our goal is to be as transparent as possible and to minimise risk — but zero risk cannot be guaranteed after optimisation.

Estimated savings compare ZendIQ's quoted output against Jupiter's concurrent live quote for the same pair and amount. Risk scores and estimated loss figures are derived from real on-chain data.
